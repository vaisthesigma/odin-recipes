Whipped Coffee

Description:

Whipped coffee, my latest obsession, is like a caffeinated cloud in a cup. It's the art of vigorously whisking together instant coffee, sugar, and hot water until it transforms into a frothy, creamy masterpiece. This rich, velvety delight sits on milk like a caffeinated crown, indulgence in every sip.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons hot water(28 g)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar(24 g)
  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee powder(12 g)
  • milk, to serve
  • ice, to serve

Steps:

  1. Add the hot water, sugar, and instant coffee to a bowl.

  2. Either hand whisk or use an electric mixer until the mixture is fluffy and light.

  3. To serve, spoon a dollop over a cup of milk with ice in it and stir.

  4. Enjoy!
