Vegan Ramen

credits for the image: Image by Freepik

Description:

In my vegan ramen creation, I've crafted a bowl of pure comfort. The broth, simmered with aromatic herbs and spices, cradles tender noodles and an array of fresh, vibrant vegetables. Topped with savory tofu and garnished with love, it's a satisfying, homemade masterpiece that nourishes the soul and delights the taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 6 cups vegetable stock(1.4 L)
  • 1 cup shiitake mushroom(75 g), stems removed and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon miso paste
  • ramen noodle

Steps:

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic, ginger, and onions and saut√© until onions become translucent.

  2. Add vegetable stock, water, shiitake mushrooms, and soy sauce. Stir and simmer for at least 45 minutes, up to 3 hours. The longer you simmer, the more flavorful the broth will become.

  3. Uncover the pot, add the miso paste, and stir.

  4. Ladle the broth into a bowl and add noodles and toppings of your choice.

  5. Enjoy!
